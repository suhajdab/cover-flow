How to find your RSS feed URL:

  1. Go to your Goodreads profile
  2. Click on a bookshelf (e.g., "Read", "Currently Reading")
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the page
  4. In the bottom page info section on the right side, look for the RSS icon
  5. Click the RSS icon to get your feed URL

Example: https://www.goodreads.com/review/list_rss/18906657?key=...&shelf=read
